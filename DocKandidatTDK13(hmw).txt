Fantasy Premier League (FPL) is an official game
organised by The Football Association, the Football Association of the United Kingdom,
Premier League promotion event as the United Kingdom. FPL is played by selecting
15 soccer player who played in the Premier League any United Kingdom pekannya
with certain rules, to maximize the solidary points
the game was actually in Manchester United Kingdom. In this game, the
players that FPL, hereinafter referred to as the Manager of FPL, tested the ability of the
its analysis in determining the expected players points
high in every game.
The genetic algorithm is one of the soft computing method
It is often used in making projections. In this research, used
genetic algorithm for projecting a player's points of FPL, and then
compare the fit with the methods already exists. Fitness function
used was the mean squared error of a linear regression modeling
Double. There are three factors which are modelled as multiple linear regression,
that is the level of difficulty of the game, the player's points average FPL in 30 days
the last, and the average player rating FPL in the last 30 days.
